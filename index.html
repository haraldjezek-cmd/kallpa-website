Everything you need to train smarter

Built on exercise science. Powered by AI. Designed for lifters who take their training seriously.

Three Ways to Build

Build fully manually, use Smart Build to get an AI-generated program tailored to your goal and level, or pick from pre-designed programs and make them your own.

AI Coaching

7 types of coaching events: plateau alerts, deload recommendations, consistency wins, and more. The AI learns your patterns and adapts.

Offline-First

Train anywhere. All data lives on your device first, syncs to the cloud when you're back online.

Volume Tracking

Track sets per muscle group against science-based MEV/MAV/MRV landmarks. Know exactly when to push and when to back off.

Bilingual

Full English and Spanish support throughout the app — not just the UI, but the AI coaching messages too.

Progress Insights

e1RM tracking, sparkline trends, and weekly summaries that tell you what actually happened — not just what you logged.

Three steps to smarter training

From setup to your first coached week in minutes.

1

Set up your program

Build from scratch, use Smart Build to generate a program from your goal and experience level, or start from a pre-designed template.

2

Train and log

Log sets, weight, and RIR (reps in reserve). The app tracks your effort and recovery in real time.

3

Get coached

After each week, your AI coach reviews your data and surfaces insights — deload when you need it, push when you're ready.
